************Cloud Computing Notes *************

Definition : utilising the cloud services such as storage, network ,Databases through internet is called Cloud computing.

Two types :
	1. Service Models
	2. Deployment Models

Service Models :

	1. IAAS (Infrastructure As a service)
	2. PAAS (Platform As a Service)
	3. SAAS (Software As a Service)

Deployment Models :
	1. Public
	2. Private
	3. Hybrid 
	4. Community
